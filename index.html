Empowering student communities with tokenomics

What

We are an umbrella organization of student communities creating a network with companies, building a model that incentivises for work.

Why

We believe the work outside plain academics matters and shapes up career directions and we needed an ecosystem of such communities on a global scale serving as a gateway for collaboration among them and companies.
We solve this.

How

We will achieve this by launching our token serving as our primary treasury, which then along with companies funds the student communities.
